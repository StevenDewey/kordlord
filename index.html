Our Promise

Say Goodbye to Tangled Earbuds.

Our Vision

Everyone is unique. Everyone has different tastes in music, videos, and all forms of entertainment. We all carry around earbuds to enjoy our lives in our own unique way. And now dealing with earbuds in a tangled mess has become a normal part of everyday life.

Why the Kord Lord?

There is a limit to how many knots a pair of earbuds can be tangled in before a line has been crossed. A few months ago we crossed that line. We wanted to create a earbud cord manager that was easy to use and something that fit our lifestyle.
