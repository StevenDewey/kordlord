Size

The diameter is big enough you won’t spend half your day rolling up your cord, and its big enough you won’t damage your cord by winding tightly around a small diameter. The bigger size also allows your earbuds to be protected in the core rather than dangling on the outside of the product, keeping you from having to replace your earbuds when the cord gets ruined or the earbuds get banged up. When earbuds are wrapped tightly around a small diameter and then stored for any length of time, when they are un-wound they immediately try revert back to their previous position. Because of the bigger diameter the Kord Lord helps to relieve this issue.

The Kord Lord is small enough to fit in your pocket, but honestly if you are wearing skinny jeans it will be a tight fit. The Kord Lord was designed to be slightly bigger for protection for your earbuds and cord and to cut down on winding time, and because of the bigger size the Kord Lord works great in bags, purses, and coat pockets. The Kord Lord will fit comfortably in most pants pockets as well, but if you wear skinny jeans, consider yourself warned. The Kord Lord is also sized to be used with any standard usb docking or charging cord. The dimples on either side of the Kord Lord give your fingers a place to settle as you unwind your cord when you're ready to use it.
