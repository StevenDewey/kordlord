Our Story

The Kord Lord

We are in an interesting phase of life. We are in our mid twenties, we are transitioning from the carefree lifestyle of our late teens and early twenties into our professional careers. We desperately want to hang onto as much of our unique personality and style as we transition into professional adulthood.

So we set out to build the Kord Lord. We wanted a earbud cord manager that would fit into our personality whether we were at work, or at the skatepark. With the sleek ergonomic design it feels good in your hand and looks classy on your desk. Yet at the same time we would not want use it, if it was more difficult than dealing with tangled earbuds.

Six prototypes later, we started to get excited about the look and the functionality of our design, after three more iterations we had a product we think all of you will like.

Magnets

The trick with designing a good product that we would want to use is to make sure it wouldn't be as difficult or more difficult to work with than untangling earbuds.

One day it hit us, “all speakers have a magnetic pull.” We tried placing our earbuds near magnets are were impressed with the strength of the connection and instantly knew magnets would be included in our final design.(Don't worry from our testing and personal use the magnets have had no effect on the performance of the earbuds)

The embedded magnets allow for easy use, simply drop your earbuds into the core of the Kord Lord and the magnets will keep your earbuds secured in the core, then wrap your cord around the outside.
